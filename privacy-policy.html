moriyatee (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "us") has established this Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as "this Policy") regarding the handling of users' personal information in the application "Hitokoto" (hereinafter referred to as "this App") provided by us.

Article 1 (Information Collected and Purpose of Use)

This App collects and uses the following information for the respective purposes:

1. Diary Data

  • Information Collected: Diary text entered by the user (up to 100 characters), mood and weather stamps, and date/time of entry
  • Storage Method: Stored in the device's local database (SQLite)
  • Purpose of Use: To provide diary display, search, statistics, calendar view, timeline view, and "On This Day" features.

2. Image Data

  • Information Collected: Images selected by the user from the camera or photo library
  • Storage Method: Stored in the app's dedicated storage area on the device
  • Purpose of Use: To provide the photo attachment feature for diary entries.

3. Settings Data

  • Information Collected: Reminder notification on/off settings, notification time
  • Storage Method: Stored in the device's settings area (SharedPreferences)
  • Purpose of Use: To provide the reminder notification feature.

This App does not transmit user data to external servers, use it for advertising purposes, or sell it to third parties. All data is stored solely on the user's device.

Article 2 (External Transmission of Information and Provision to Third Parties)

This App does not transmit user data externally. All data is stored on the user's device and cannot be accessed by us.

However, when users use the backup feature to export data, the privacy policies of the export destination services (iCloud Drive, Google Drive, etc.) will apply. Export is performed only by user action.

Article 3 (Access to Device Features)

This App may request access to the following device features. Access is granted only with the user's permission:

  • Camera: To take photos for diary attachments
  • Photo Library: To select images for diary attachments
  • Notifications: To send reminder notifications

Article 4 (Security Measures)

All data in this App is stored on the user's device, so data security depends on the device's security features. We strive to handle data safely in the design of the application.

Article 5 (Changes to Privacy Policy)

We may change this Policy as necessary. However, if changes to this Policy require user consent under applicable laws, the changed Policy will apply only to users who have consented to the changes in the manner prescribed by us. When changing this Policy, we will notify users of the effective date and content of the changed Policy by displaying it on our website or other appropriate means.

Article 6 (Contact Information)

For opinions, questions, complaints, or other inquiries regarding the handling of user information, please contact us at the following:
